About Us

"Your e-business Solutions Partner"

Get to know us.

We've been in business since 2001 and are committed to our customers.

Who We Are.

Silicon Mountain Technologies is a software development firm established in 2001. We have enjoyed organic growth by creating long-term strategic relationships through ethical business practices, fixed pricing, and quality solutions. Over the years our enterprise software development has given customers confidence in SMT’s expertise, honesty and dependability.

Dedication.

SMT has become a leading complex-web solutions provider for Fortune 500 companies with a focus on the medical manufacturing industry and the franchise market space. Utilizing our Web Crescendo™3's modular design, customers enjoy a robust software package that includes Integrated Marketing, Customer Profile, Web Content, and Learning Management Systems. SMT’s innovation doesn’t stop with WebCrescendo™, but continues with constant dedication to producing cutting-edge software while managing our customers' individual needs.

What We Do.

Web Crescendo's™ third generation delivers all of this in our competitive cost structured approach, along with secured dual-hosting, training for customer website modifications, technical support, and access to our experienced staff for more advanced development. SMT also offers consulting and development services based on specific customer needs. SMT creates user environments that provide tools for companies to control their online strategy or utilize our internal engineers for more in-depth enhancements. (We don’t hold our customers hostage.)
